Rules of the Game

Playing is quite simple:

  • At the start of the game, you can pick the difficulty and category of questions or have one selected for you at random
  • There are a variety of categories and difficulty ranges from Easy to Medium to Hard
  • At the start your turn, you will be presented with a question based on the chosen category
  • If you answer correctly, you get to pick a position on the board
  • If you don't, you lose your turn
  • First player to line up four slots, wins the round!
