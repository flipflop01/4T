Rules of the Game

Playing is quite simple:

  • One player, selected randomly every round, gets to pick the category and difficulty of the questions
  • There are a variety of categories and difficulty ranges from Easy to Medium to Hard
  • At the start your turn, you pick a position on the grid that you'd like to occupy
  • But in order to take that position you have to answer the corresponding question correctly
  • If you fail to answer correctly, you lose your turn
  • First person to line up three slots, wins the round!
