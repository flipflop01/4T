Rules of the Game

Playing is quite simple:

  • At the start of the game, you pick the category of questions
  • The difficulty of questions is randomly selected between Easy, Medium, and Hard
  • At the start your turn, you will be presented with a question based on the chosen category
  • If you answer correctly, you get to pick a position on the board. After each of your moves the board will be disabled and you move on to the next question
  • If you answer incorrectly, no moves can be made and you must move on to the next question
  • Line up three moves, and you win the round!
